# Code of conduct

sluice is a small, security-focused project. We want taking part - filing issues, sending
patches, asking questions - to be a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity.

## Expected

- Be considerate and constructive; assume good faith and keep criticism about the code.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences, and accept feedback gracefully.

## Not acceptable

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory or demeaning remarks.
- Unwelcome personal or sexual attention.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussion, or other unprofessional conduct.

## Scope and enforcement

This applies in all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and when representing the
project in public. Report concerns privately to <kan.nam.dev@gmail.com>; reports are handled
discreetly. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, or restrict participation, for behavior
they judge inappropriate.

A condensed policy in the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
